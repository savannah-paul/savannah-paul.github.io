Description: Meet for 2 hours each week to participate in a group role play game.


Embark on a journey where imagination knows no bounds! Join us this June for a story-rich Dungeons & Dragons summer camp where adventurers of all experience levels come together to explore magical realms, forge new friendships, face thrilling challenges, discover ancient mysteries, and create epic tales of bravery and heroism!

Whether you’re a daring knight, a clever rogue, a powerful mage—or something entirely your own—you’ll join a party of fellow storytellers to roll the dice, build worlds, and bring your character to life in unforgettable ways.

  • Sessions will be held on most Saturdays in June and July at the Boulder public library. We’ll meet in a private room.
  • Over the course of six sessions, our experienced Dungeon Masters will teach the basics of Dungeons & Dragons, guide players through character creation, and lead them through a complete story.
  • We’ll provide a light snack each session, along with all the materials needed to play. Campers are welcome to bring their own dice, books, or character sheets if they’d like.
  • All experience levels are welcome!
