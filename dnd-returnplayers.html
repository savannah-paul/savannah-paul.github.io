Continue the Quest: Dungeons & Dragons Fall Adventure


For returning summer adventurers only


Ages: 8-12

Cost: $225

Description: Meet on Saturdays throughout September, October, and November in a reserved room at the Boulder Public Library.

The journey doesn’t end with summer! Rejoin your fellow heroes for the next thrilling chapter in our ongoing Dungeons & Dragons campaign. Designed exclusively for returning players, this fall session picks up where Curse of the Cloud King left off—bringing back familiar characters, deepening the story, and revealing new realms of danger, mystery, and magic.

Over the course of six Saturday sessions at the Boulder Public Library, you’ll reunite with your adventuring party and dive even further into the world you've helped save. Expect more challenging encounters, more opportunities for creative roleplaying, and brand-new twists as your Dungeon Master guides you through an original continuation of your summer campaign.

We’ll continue to provide all materials needed to play, along with light snacks each session. Players are welcome to bring their own dice, character sheets, or gear from the summer session.
