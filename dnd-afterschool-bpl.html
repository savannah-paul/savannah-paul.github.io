Dungeons and Dragons After School


Ages: 8-12
Time: 4-6PM
Location: Boulder Public Library - Main Branch
Cost: $225
Sessions: 6
Session Dates:
— September 2
— September 9
— September 16
— September 23
— September 30
— October 7
Description: Meet for 2 hours on Tuesdays throughout September and October to participate in a group role play game.

Embark on a journey where imagination knows no bounds! Join us this Fall for a story-rich Dungeons & Dragons campaign where adventurers of all experience levels come together to explore magical realms, forge new friendships, face thrilling challenges, and create epic tales of bravery and heroism!

Whether you’re a daring knight, a clever rogue, a powerful mage—or something entirely your own—you’ll join a party of fellow storytellers to roll the dice, build worlds, and bring your character to life in unforgettable ways.

  • Sessions will be held after school on Tuesdays in September and October at the Boulder Public Library in a reserved room.
  • Our experienced Dungeon Masters will teach the basics of Dungeons & Dragons, guide players through character creation, and lead them through a complete story.
  • We’ll provide a light snack each session, along with all the materials needed to play. Kids are welcome to bring their own dice, books, or character sheets if they’d like.
  • All experience levels are welcome!

* We aim to make group placement as fun and comfortable as possible. If you feel your child would thrive in a different group than their age range suggests, please don’t hesitate to reach out—we’re happy to be flexible.
* We believe every adventurer should have access to this experience. Payment plans and financial assistance are available—please contact us if cost is a concern.
