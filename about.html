About Us


At Grimoire Games, we believe in the power of storytelling, imagination, and a well-rolled d20. We specialize in running Dungeons & Dragons sessions designed just for kids, combining adventure and creativity in a safe, engaging environment.

Our team has years of experience working with kids, both as after-school instructors and as organizers of a dedicated Dungeons & Dragons summer camp. We've guided young heroes through quests and know how to tailor every adventure to suit their age, interests, and energy.

We're currently partnering with schools to lead after-school programs, and also host standalone Dungeons & Dragons sessions.

Whether your child is brand new to the world of fantasy role-playing or a seasoned young adventurer, Grimoire Games is here to help them embark on unforgettable journeys—one roll at a time.
