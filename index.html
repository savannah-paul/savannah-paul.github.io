Grimoire Games hosts fun, welcoming spaces where kids can dive into the imaginative world of Dungeons & Dragons! Whether they're brand new to the game or already have a few adventures under their belt, our camp makes learning and playing D&D easy and exciting for everyone.

Each week, kids will gather for two hours of collaborative storytelling, problem-solving, and creative play. Our experienced game masters provide everything needed—including character sheets, dice, maps, and minis—so campers can focus on making friends and having fun.

Through the magic of tabletop roleplaying, campers will build confidence, strengthen their math and reading skills, and learn how to work as a team—all while embarking on epic quests and unforgettable adventures.

FAQ

Dungeons & Dragons (or D&D) is a tabletop roleplaying game. That means it’s played like a board game, but instead of a fixed board and pieces, most of the action takes place in our imaginations! Players create characters and work together to tell a story, making choices along the way. Will you fight the evil king or convince him to change his ways? Face the mighty dragon or sneak past it? In D&D, you decide what happens next!
