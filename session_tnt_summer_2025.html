Towers and Tiaras: Summer Camp 2025

Ages: 8-12

Time: 2:30-4:30PM

Location: Boulder Public Library - Main Branch

Cost: $220

Sessions: 6

Session Dates:

— June 14th
— June 21st
— June 28th
— July 12th
— July 19th
— July 26th

**We are not meeting 4th of July weekend

Description: Meet for 2 hours each week to participate in a group role play game.


Step into a world of enchanted forests, daring quests, magical creatures, and legendary heroines. Towers & Tiaras is a story-driven Dungeons & Dragons summer camp where adventurers craft characters, conquer challenges, and shape epic tales in realms where castles sparkle and courage reigns. From royal intrigues to enchanted beasts, each campaign is designed to celebrate imagination, friendship, and fearless fun.

Perfect for first-time players and seasoned heroes alike, this camp welcomes anyone who loves a little glitter with their dragons.

  • Sessions will be held on most Saturdays in June and July at the Boulder public library. We’ll meet in a private room.
  • Over the course of six sessions, our experienced Dungeon Masters will teach the basics of Dungeons & Dragons, guide players through character creation, and lead them through a complete story.
  • We’ll provide a light snack each session, along with all the materials needed to play. Campers are welcome to bring their own dice, books, or character sheets if they’d like.
  • All experience levels are welcome!
